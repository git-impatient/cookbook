# Chocolate Chip Banoffee Pie 

*Source : [Foodista](https://www.foodista.com/recipe/T2GX4HBN/chocolate-chip-banoffee-pie)*

## Ingredients
- 300.0 gram double chocolate chip cookies
- 100.0 gram butter
- 50.0 gram whole hazelnuts
- 100.0 gram butter
- 100.0 gram sugar
- 375.0 gram caramel condensed milk
- 4.0 medium very ripe bananas, sliced
- 0.5 lemon juice
- 300.0 milliliter fresh cream
- 1.0 tablespoon dusting sugar
vanilla extract(optional)
- 50.0 gram grated dark chocolate

![Image of a Banoffee pie](recipes/photo_banoffee.jpg)
*Source de l'image : [Cookidoo](https://cookidoo.fr/recipes/recipe/fr-FR/r211888)*

## Instructions


- Step 1
Melt the butter.


- Step 2
Roast the hazelnuts in a clean hot pan.


- Step 3
Crush the cookies.

- Step 4
Mix the melted butter, crushed cookies, and roasted hazelnuts together. Press the mixture to cover the bottom of a greased and lined 23 cm spring form cake tin with baking paper.


- Step 5
Chill the crust while you prepare the caramel cream.


- Step 6
1 minute
Heat the sugar and butter in a saucepan until the sugar is completely dissolved and starts to change color. Carefully add the caramel condensed milk, bring to a boil for one minute, then remove from heat.

- Step 7
1 hour
Pour the caramel sauce over the chilled cake base and let it chill again for at least 1 hour.

- Step 8
Sprinkle the lemon juice over the sliced bananas.

- Step 9
Whip the fresh cream and fold in half of the sliced bananas.

- Step 10
Spread the whipped cream and banana mixture over the caramel layer on the cake.

- Step 11
Decorate the top with the remaining sliced bananas and cover with grated dark chocolate.

- Step 12
1 hour
If possible, chill for another hour before removing the cake from the spring form tin and serving.
