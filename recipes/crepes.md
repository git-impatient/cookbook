##* Leave the light side of the pankakes and join the dark side of the French crepes *

Crêpes are very thin pancakes that can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings. The dish dates back to at least 13th-century France, and remains a breakfast and brunch favorite around the world.

Ingredients for TRUE Britany Crêpes

Crêpes are easy to make with basic ingredients you likely already have on hand:

![image of crepe](recipes/crepe_image.jpg)

Froment Flour: These basic French crêpes call for a cup of all-purpose flour. : 250 grams of T70

Sarasin Flour : 1 big spoon (gives the crepes some character !)

Eggs: Eggs act as a binder, which means they help hold the batter together you will need 3

Milk: Milk adds moisture and keeps the crêpes tender 480 mL

Water: Water helps thin the batter : 100 mL

Salt: A small spoon of salt enhances the overall flavor.

Oil or Butter but for this one Oil is gucci !
